L8: Overtourism in Barcelona — 巴塞隆納的過度旅遊

課文逐句精講
句法標色說明： S 主詞  V 動詞  O 受詞  C 補語  M 修飾語  點擊每句可展開中文翻譯與講解。
►In this busy European city, large groups of tourists are everywhere.
在這座繁忙的歐洲城市，大批觀光客無處不在。
句法分析 M介片 In this busy European city， S主詞 large groups of tourists V動詞 are C補語 everywhere
開篇倒裝效果：介系詞片語 In this busy European city 移至句首，製造懸疑感。large groups of 強調數量眾多。
►They overflow the sidewalks and streets, taking pictures at landmarks like the "Sagrada Familia" and crowding narrow passageways leading to markets.
他們擠滿人行道和街道，在「聖家堂」等地標拍照，並擠進通往市場的狹窄通道。
句法分析 S They V overflow O the sidewalks and streets， M分詞構句 taking pictures… and crowding…
overflow (v.) 溢出；擠滿（本課關鍵動詞）。
分詞構句：taking… and crowding… 為伴隨狀態，主詞同為 They。
passageways leading to markets：現在分詞片語 leading to markets 後位修飾 passageways。
►The city in question is Barcelona, Spain, which each year receives more than fifteen times as many visitors as its actual population!
討論中的這座城市，正是西班牙巴塞隆納，它每年接待的遊客人數超過其實際人口的十五倍！
句法分析 S The city in question V is C Barcelona, Spain， M關係子句 which each year receives more than fifteen times as many visitors as its actual population
in question：片語，意為「討論中的；正提到的」。
倍數比較：fifteen times as many visitors as… = 遊客數是…的十五倍。
which 為非限制性關係代名詞，代替整個地名 Barcelona, Spain。
►Barcelona is suffering from a phenomenon called "overtourism."
巴塞隆納正深受一種稱為「過度旅遊」的現象所苦。
句法分析 S Barcelona V is suffering from O a phenomenon called "overtourism"
suffer from：受苦於；深受…困擾（固定片語）。
called "overtourism"：過去分詞片語後位修飾 phenomenon。
►This occurs when an attraction becomes overrun with tourists to such an extent that the local residents' quality of life declines dramatically.
當一個景點被遊客擠得不堪重負，嚴重到當地居民的生活品質大幅下滑時，這種現象就會發生。
句法分析 S This V occurs M時間子句 when an attraction becomes overrun with tourists M程度/結果 to such an extent that the local residents' quality of life declines dramatically
to such an extent that…：到…的程度（結果子句）。
decline dramatically：大幅下滑（decline = 下降，dramatically = 大幅地）。
overrun with：被…占滿/淹沒。
►The 1992 Olympics put Barcelona on the tourist map, and soon afterward the number of tourists flocking to the city began to increase.
1992 年奧運會將巴塞隆納推向了觀光地圖，此後不久，湧入這座城市的觀光客數量開始增加。
句法分析 S The 1992 Olympics V put O Barcelona M on the tourist map； S the number of tourists flocking to the city V began to increase
put … on the tourist map：使…成為知名旅遊地（慣用表達）。
flock (v.)：群集、蜂擁。tourists flocking to the city = 現在分詞片語修飾 tourists。
►In 2017, the number of annual visitors to Barcelona grew to almost five times as large as that in 1992.
2017 年，巴塞隆納的年度遊客人數增長到幾乎是 1992 年的五倍。
句法分析 M In 2017， S the number of annual visitors to Barcelona V grew M to almost five times as large as that in 1992
five times as large as that in 1992：倍數比較句。that 代替前面的 the number，避免重複。
公式：數字 + times + as + adj./adv. + as（= 是…的幾倍）。
►As expected, local hotels and businesses benefited from this expansion of tourism.
不出所料，當地飯店和商家從旅遊業的擴張中獲益。
句法分析 M As expected， S local hotels and businesses V benefited from O this expansion of tourism
As expected：如預期般（慣用語）。
expansion (n.)：擴張；expand (v.) 擴大。benefit from：從…獲益。
►However, the city's residents had to bear the cost.
然而，市民卻不得不承擔代價。
句法分析 M However， S the city's residents V had to bear O the cost
bear the cost：承擔代價（= pay the price）。此句為轉折，引出後段的負面影響。
►This invasion of visitors has resulted in various problems.
這股訪客入侵已造成各種問題。
句法分析 S This invasion of visitors V has resulted in O various problems
invasion (n.)：入侵；invade (v.) 入侵。此處隱喻性用法，把觀光客比喻為「入侵者」。
result in：導致（= lead to）。
►Complaints about problems caused by thoughtless visitors are common.
對於無禮遊客所造成問題的抱怨十分普遍。
句法分析 S Complaints about problems caused by thoughtless visitors V are C common
complaint (n.)：抱怨；complain (v.) 抱怨。
thoughtless (adj.)：欠缺考慮的；不體貼的。thought (想法) + -less (無) = 無思考的。
►"These people have no respect for others," said Emilio Shasta, a local who has lived in Barcelona his entire life.
「這些人完全不尊重他人，」當地居民 Emilio Shasta 說，他一生都住在巴塞隆納。
句法分析（引述句） 直接引語：S These people V have no respect M for others。
倒裝引述：V said S Emilio Shasta（動詞前置）。
have no respect for：完全不尊重…。
who has lived in Barcelona his entire life：限制性關係子句修飾 a local。
►They play loud music late at night and throw garbage all over the place.
他們在深夜大聲播放音樂，並將垃圾扔得到處都是。
句法分析 S They V play O loud music M late at night； V throw O garbage M all over the place
all over the place：到處（慣用語）。late at night：深夜（固定搭配）。
►Residents are also fed up with some visitors' inappropriate behavior, such as urinating and throwing up on streets outside nightclubs.
居民也對某些遊客的不當行為感到厭煩，例如在夜店外的街道上小便和嘔吐。
句法分析 S Residents V are C fed up with some visitors' inappropriate behavior， M舉例 such as urinating and throwing up…
be fed up with：對…感到厭煩（片語；= be tired of / be sick of）。
throw up：嘔吐（= vomit）。
such as：例如（後接名詞或動名詞）。
►Furthermore, some local tenants have even been forced to leave their apartments because landlords are partial to renting their places out through Airbnb.
此外，部分當地租客甚至被迫離開他們的公寓，因為房東偏好透過 Airbnb 出租房屋。
句法分析 M Furthermore， S some local tenants V have been forced to leave O their apartments， M原因子句 because landlords are partial to renting their places out through Airbnb
tenant (n.)：房客；landlord (n.)：房東。
be partial to + V-ing/N：偏愛；偏袒（partial = 偏心的）。
have been forced to：被迫（被動語態完成式）。
►In this way, landlords can make more money by charging tourists higher nightly rents.
如此一來，房東可以向遊客收取更高的每晚租金，從中賺取更多錢。
句法分析 M In this way， S landlords V can make O more money M by charging tourists higher nightly rents
by + V-ing：藉由…（表方法的介系詞）。nightly：每夜的（adj./adv.）。
►"Many residents of this area are so sick and tired of tourists that they have decided to leave." Even I myself am also considering that option.
「這個地區的許多居民已對觀光客深感厭倦，以至於決定離開。」就連我自己也在考慮這個選擇。
句法分析 引語：S Many residents V are so sick and tired of tourists M結果子句 that they have decided to leave；
M Even S I myself V am considering O that option
so + adj. + that…：如此…以至於（結果子句）。
sick and tired of：對…深感厭倦（= fed up with，語氣更強）。
myself：反身代名詞，加強語氣（強調主詞 I）。option (n.)：選項、選擇。
►The good news is that city officials are keenly aware of all these complaints.
好消息是，市政官員對這些抱怨十分清楚。
句法分析 S The good news V is Cthat子句 that city officials are keenly aware of all these complaints
keenly aware of：十分清楚/意識到（keenly = 非常，強調程度）。
►They believe that only by introducing practical measures can the impact of overtourism be reduced.
他們認為，唯有引進實際措施，過度旅遊的衝擊才能被減輕。
句法分析（倒裝句） S They V believe（主句）；
Monly by…倒裝 only by introducing practical measures V can（助動詞前移） S the impact of overtourism V be reduced
only by + V-ing 倒裝：以 only + 副詞修飾語開頭時，主句須倒裝（助動詞提前）。
measure (n.)：措施、方法（可數名詞）；practical：實際可行的。
►Such measures include putting limits on the number of people who can visit certain hotspots at a time, and banning large tour groups in particularly popular areas.
這些措施包括：限制每次可前往特定熱門景點的人數，以及禁止大型旅遊團進入特別受歡迎的地區。
句法分析 S Such measures V include O putting limits on… and banning…（兩個動名詞片語並列）
put a limit on：對…加以限制。
hotspot：熱門景點、熱點。at a time：每次、一次（= at one time）。
►The city has also restricted the development of new hotels and ordered that websites like Airbnb take down their listings of guesthouses that are not licensed.
該市還限制新飯店的開發，並命令 Airbnb 等網站下架未獲授權的民宿清單。
句法分析 S The city V has restricted O the development of new hotels； V ordered Othat子句 that websites like Airbnb take down their listings…
take down：撤下；拆除（此指下架）。
order + that + S + V原形：命令…（假設語氣，that 子句動詞用原形）。
licensed (adj.)：有執照的（license 的形容詞）。
►Finally, the government has come up with a thorough plan that concentrates less on promotion and more on tourism management.
最後，政府提出了一個全面性計畫，著重於旅遊管理而非旅遊推廣。
句法分析 M Finally， S the government V has come up with O a thorough plan M關係子句 that concentrates less on promotion and more on tourism management
come up with：提出；想出（= devise / develop）。
thorough (adj.)：徹底的、全面的。
concentrate (v.)：專注；集中（在此為「著重於」）。less… more…：較少…較多…（對比結構）。
►It has been said that tourism is a double-edged sword.
人們常說，旅遊業是一把雙刃劍。
句法分析 S It（虛主詞） V has been said Cthat子句 that tourism is a double-edged sword
It has been said that…：人們常說…（被動語態 + 虛主詞）。double-edged sword：雙刃劍（比喻有利有弊）。
►On the one hand, tourism provides local business owners with great economic benefits. On the other hand, it disturbs the lives of local residents and can cause numerous social problems.
一方面，旅遊業為當地商家帶來巨大的經濟利益。另一方面，它擾亂了當地居民的生活，並可能造成眾多社會問題。
句法分析 M On the one hand… On the other hand（對比結構）。
S tourism V provides O local business owners M with great economic benefits（provide sb. with sth.）。
provide sb. with sth.：提供某人某物（= provide sth. for sb.）。on the one hand… on the other hand：一方面…另一方面（正式對比表達）。
►Clearly, Barcelona is striving to deal with the negative aspects of tourism and is acting to strike a balance between the local community and international visitors.
顯然，巴塞隆納正努力應對旅遊業的負面影響，並採取行動在當地社區與國際訪客之間取得平衡。
句法分析 M Clearly， S Barcelona V is striving to deal with…； V is acting to strike a balance between…
strive (v.)：努力、奮鬥（三態：strive-strove-striven）。
strike a balance between A and B：在 A 與 B 之間取得平衡（重要片語）。
►Only after the authorities have developed sustainable solutions will overtourism be manageable and stop causing problems in society.
唯有當局制定出永續解決方案後，過度旅遊才能變得可控，並不再對社會造成問題。
句法分析（倒裝句） Monly after…倒裝 Only after the authorities have developed sustainable solutions V will（助動詞前移） S overtourism C be manageable and stop causing problems in society
Only after + 子句 + 倒裝：否定副詞詞組置首，主句助動詞前移。
sustainable (adj.)：永續的；manageable (adj.)：可管理的、可控的。

片語講解 x 7
片語 中文 例句 & 說明
in question 討論中的；正提到的
The city in question is Barcelona.討論中的城市就是巴塞隆納。
用於名詞後，修飾「正在談論的」事物，無需冠詞。
to such an extent that 到…的程度（以至於）
The noise was to such an extent that neighbors called the police.噪音嚴重到鄰居報警。
= so... that...。結果子句，強調嚴重程度。
be fed up with 對…感到厭煩
She was fed up with tourists blocking her doorway every morning.她對每天早晨遊客堵住她門口感到厭煩。
= be sick and tired of / be tired of。後接 N 或 V-ing。
throw up 嘔吐
Some visitors even threw up on the streets after drinking.部分遊客喝酒後甚至在街上嘔吐。
= vomit。口語化用法。另義：「建造」(throw up a building)。
take down 撤下；拆除；記下
The city ordered Airbnb to take down unlicensed listings.市政府命令 Airbnb 下架未獲授權的房源。
可分離片語（受詞可夾中間）：take it down / take down the listing。
put a limit on 對…加以限制
Officials decided to put a limit on daily visitor numbers.官員決定對每日遊客人數加以限制。
= set a limit on / restrict。名詞 limit 可複數：put limits on。
strike a balance between 在…之間取得平衡
The city is trying to strike a balance between tourism and residents' needs.該市正試圖在旅遊業與居民需求之間取得平衡。
= find a balance between。strike（打擊）→ 比喻「找到」平衡點。

文法句型 x 3
1. 否定副詞詞組倒裝句：Only after / Only by + 倒裝 ▼
Only after + S + V, 助動詞 + S + V (原形) Only by + V-ing, 助動詞 + S + V (原形)

當 only 修飾的副詞詞組（含 after / by / when / if 等）移至句首時，後面的主句須做「倒裝」——助動詞提前到主詞前。

課文例句一：
Only after the authorities have developed sustainable solutions will overtourism be manageable.
唯有當局制定出永續解決方案後，過度旅遊才能變得可控。
課文例句二：
They believe that only by introducing practical measures can the impact of overtourism be reduced.
他們認為，唯有引進實際措施，過度旅遊的衝擊才能被減輕。
額外練習：
Only when we respect the environment can we ensure a sustainable future.
唯有尊重環境，我們才能確保永續的未來。
常見否定詞組倒裝格式
Only after + 子句助動詞 + S + V
Only by + V-ing助動詞 + S + V
Only when + 子句助動詞 + S + V
Never / Seldom / Rarely助動詞 + S + V
句首 Only 修飾的不是主詞時才倒裝。若 Only I know...（Only 修飾主詞 I），不需倒裝。
學測閱讀常見：Not until... did...（直到…才…）也是相同倒裝邏輯。
2. 強調句型：It is/was... that/who... ▼
It is/was + 被強調成分 + that/who + 句子其餘部分

將想強調的成分（主詞、受詞、副詞）移至 It is/was 後，再接 that/who 連接剩餘句子。

強調地點（概念例句）：
It is Barcelona that receives more than fifteen times as many visitors as its actual population.
正是巴塞隆納，每年接待的遊客超過其人口的十五倍。
強調主詞（人）：
It was the landlords who forced tenants to leave.
正是那些房東迫使租客離開的。
強調時間：
It was in 1992 that the Olympics put Barcelona on the tourist map.
正是在 1992 年，奧運會使巴塞隆納成為旅遊名城。
強調成分連接詞例
主詞（人）who / thatIt was John who called.
主詞（物）thatIt was the storm that caused the damage.
受詞 / 副詞thatIt is hard work that matters.
強調句移除 It is/was...that 後，剩餘句子仍語法完整。若不完整則可能是「虛主詞」句，非強調句。
學測常考：分辨 It is...that（強調句）與 It is important that...（虛主詞）的結構差異。
3. 倍數比較句：數字 + times + as ... as ▼
數字 + times + as + adj./adv. + as + 比較對象 數字 + times + the + 名詞（size / number / amount...）+ of + 比較對象

表達「是…的幾倍」時，英文常用 倍數 + as … as 的結構。

課文例句一：
Barcelona receives more than fifteen times as many visitors as its actual population.
巴塞隆納每年接待的遊客超過其實際人口的十五倍。
課文例句二：
The number of visitors grew to almost five times as large as that in 1992.
遊客人數增長到幾乎是 1992 年的五倍。
另一常見格式：
Taiwan is about twice the size of the island of Kyushu.
台灣大約是九州島的兩倍大。
格式說明
