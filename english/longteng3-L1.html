課文逐句精講
句型標記： S 主詞 V 動詞 O 受詞 C 補語 M 修飾語 ── 點擊每一句展開中文翻譯、句型分析與老師解說
Para 1 ── 工作背景介紹
▶A few years ago, I was employed by the McDonald's Corporation.
幾年前，我受雇於麥當勞公司。
句型分析 A few years ago， IS was employedV（被動） by the McDonald's CorporationM（介系詞片語）。
重點：employ（雇用）的被動式 be employed by...，表示「受雇於…」。介系詞用 by 而非 from。字族：employer（雇主）、employee（員工）、employment（雇用）。
▶On "Ronald Day," I would dress up as Ronald McDonald and visit the community hospital's sick children.
在「羅納德日」，我會打扮成麥當勞叔叔，去社區醫院探視生病的孩子們。
句型分析 On "Ronald Day"， IS would dress upV（過去習慣） as Ronald McDonald and visitV the community hospital's sick childrenO。
重點：would + V 表示「過去的習慣性動作」。dress up as...「打扮成…」，介系詞用 as（強調角色/身份）。
▶Most of these young patients were suffering from terminal illnesses.
這些年輕病患中的大多數都正罹患末期疾病。
句型分析 Most of these young patientsS were sufferingV（過去進行） from terminal illnessesM。
重點：terminal（adj.）末期的，強調無法治癒。suffer from...「罹患（疾病）；受…折磨」，固定搭配介系詞 from。
▶Thus, I would try my best to ease their pain.
因此，我會盡力減輕他們的痛苦。
句型分析 Thus， IS would tryV my best to ease their painO。
重點：try one's best to V「盡力做某事」。ease（v.）減輕、緩和，常搭配 pain（痛苦）、burden（負擔）。
▶And I found it tremendously rewarding to be able to bring some laughter to the kids.
而我發現能為孩子們帶來些許歡笑是極大的回報。
句型分析 IS foundV itO（虛受詞） tremendously rewardingC（受詞補語） to be able to bring some laughter to the kids（真正受詞）。
重點：find + it + adj. + to V 虛受詞結構：it 代替後面的不定詞片語。tremendously（adv.）極大地，rewarding（adj.）有意義的。
Para 2 ── 工作規定說明
▶One of the rules of my job was never to visit the children alone when I was dressed as Ronald, for I might frighten them with my painted face and red wig.
我工作規定之一是當我打扮成羅納德時絕對不能獨自探視孩子，因為我可能會用彩妝臉孔和紅色假髮嚇到他們。
句型分析 One of the rules of my jobS wasV never to visit the children aloneC（不定詞） when I was dressed as RonaldM（時間副詞子句）， for I might frighten them...M（原因子句）。
重點：for 作連接詞（=because），語氣較正式，常見於書面。frighten（v.）使恐懼；形容詞：frightened（人感到害怕）/ frightening（令人害怕的）。
▶I wasn't permitted to have any physical contact with the children either, since doing so might pass on germs to them.
我也不被允許與孩子們有任何身體接觸，因為這樣做可能會將細菌傳給他們。
句型分析 IS wasn't permittedV（被動否定） to have any physical contact with the children， since doing so might pass on germs to themM（原因子句）。
重點：pass on...to...「傳遞…給…」，此處傳遞細菌（germs）。since 在此作「因為」（由上下文判斷）。contact（n./v.）接觸；聯絡。
▶Though I understood the reasoning behind these rules, I also felt that sometimes nothing could convey love, empathy, and warmth like a heartfelt embrace.
雖然我理解這些規定背後的道理，但我也覺得有時候沒有任何事物能像一個真誠的擁抱那樣傳遞愛、同理心和溫暖。
句型分析 Though I understood the reasoning behind these rulesM（讓步子句）， IS feltV that nothing could convey love...like a heartfelt embraceO（that 子句）。
重點：nothing...like... 「沒有任何事物像…那樣…」強調句結構。convey（v.）傳遞（情感/訊息）。empathy（同理心）vs. sympathy（同情心）：前者感同身受，後者僅同情。
Para 3 ── 遇見 Billy
▶Once, as I was leaving the hospital after finishing my work for the day, I heard a voice coming from a half-open door.
有一次，當我在結束當天工作後正要離開醫院時，我聽到一個從半開著的門傳來的聲音。
句型分析 Once， as I was leaving the hospital after finishing my work for the dayM（時間副詞子句）， IS heardV a voice coming from a half-open doorO（感官動詞+V-ing）。
重點：感官動詞 hear + O + V-ing（強調動作進行中）。as 作「當…時」，強調兩動作同時進行。coming from 為現在分詞片語修飾 voice。
▶"Ronald," the frail voice called, prompting me to push open the door and go in.
「羅納德，」那虛弱的聲音呼喚著，促使我推開門走了進去。
句型分析 the frail voiceS calledV， prompting me to push open the door and go inM（分詞構句，表結果）。
重點：prompting me to...為分詞構句，表示結果（= and it prompted me to...）。frail（adj.）虛弱的，多形容老人或病人。prompt sb. to V「促使某人做某事」。
▶I found a small boy of about five lying in bed and looking extremely weak and pale.
我發現一個大約五歲的小男孩躺在床上，看起來極度虛弱蒼白。
句型分析 IS foundV a small boy of about fiveO lying in bed and looking extremely weak and paleC（受詞補語，V-ing）。
重點：find + O + V-ing：受詞補語用現在分詞，強調「正在做」的狀態。此句與 find + O + adj. 同為學測重點，需能辨別使用時機。
▶It was really heartbreaking to see such a young boy confined to a hospital bed.
看到這麼小的男孩被困在病床上，真的令人心碎。
句型分析 ItS（虛主詞） wasV really heartbreakingC to see such a young boy confined to a hospital bed（真正主詞）。
重點：It is + adj. + to V 虛主詞結構。confine（v.）限制、使局限，confined to a hospital bed（被動），意指「只能躺在病床上」。
Para 4 ── 與 Billy 互動
▶I knew I had to do anything I could to cheer little Billy up, so I stayed and did some magic tricks for him.
我知道我必須盡我所能讓小比利振作起來，所以我留了下來並為他表演了一些魔術。
句型分析 IS knewV I had to do anything I could to cheer little Billy upO（受詞子句）； so IS stayed and didV some magic tricksO。
重點：cheer...up「使…振作」，受詞為代名詞必須放中間（cheer him up）。do anything one could「盡所能做到的一切」。
▶A sparkle soon came to his eyes and I asked if I could do anything else for him.
他的眼中很快閃現出一絲光芒，我問他我是否還能為他做些什麼。
句型分析 A sparkleS cameV to his eyes； IS askedV if I could do anything else for himO（間接問句）。
重點：sparkle（n.）閃光，come to his eyes 慣用表達，指眼中閃現光芒（恢復活力）。ask if...「詢問是否…」為間接問句，語序用直述句。
▶Although having to turn him down pained me deeply, I had no choice.
雖然不得不拒絕他讓我深感痛苦，但我別無選擇。
句型分析 Although having to turn him down pained me deeplyM（讓步子句）， IS hadV no choiceO。
重點：turn...down「拒絕」，受詞為代名詞時必須放中間（turn him down，不可 turn down him）。動名詞片語 having to turn him down 在子句中作主詞。
▶A while later, it appeared that he was getting drowsy, so I rose to my feet to get ready to leave.
過了一會兒，他似乎愈來愈昏昏欲睡，所以我起身準備離開。
句型分析 A while later， itS（虛主詞） appearedV that he was getting drowsy（真正主詞）； IS rose to my feetV（慣用語）。
重點：drowsy（adj.）昏昏欲睡的，多由疲勞或藥物引起。rose to one's feet「起身站立」。appear that...「似乎…」為正式書面語。
Para 5 ── 打破規則的決定
▶That was when he stretched out his tiny arms to hug me.
就在那時，他伸出他那細小的雙臂想要擁抱我。
句型分析 ThatS wasV when he stretched out his tiny arms to hug me（關係副詞子句）。
重點：That was when...「就在那時…」慣用說法。stretch out「伸出」，受詞可前可後：stretch out his arms / stretch his arms out。
▶I was about to refuse again, but then I saw the look of hope in his eyes.
我正要再次拒絕，但此時我看到了他眼中那充滿希望的神情。
句型分析 IS was about to refuseV（be about to = 即將）；but then IS sawV the look of hope in his eyesO。
重點：be about to + V（即將要做某事），常與 when / but then 連用，形成「正要…，就在此時…」的轉折結構。
▶Suddenly, I realized I no longer cared if I was caught breaking the rules.
突然間，我意識到我不再在乎是否會被抓到違反規則。
句型分析 IS realizedV I no longer cared if I was caught breaking the rulesO（受詞子句）。
重點：be caught + V-ing「被抓到做某事」。no longer 否定詞「不再」，置於 be 動詞/助動詞後，一般動詞前。
▶Some things in life, after all, were more important.
畢竟，生命中有些事更為重要。
句型分析 Some things in lifeS， after allM（插入語）， wereV more importantC。
重點：after all「畢竟；終究」，用作插入語（插句兩側加逗號），提醒對方考量某個理由或事實。
▶I picked him up and held him in my arms.
我把他抱起來，把他抱在懷裡。
句型分析 IS pickedV himO up and heldV himO in my arms。
重點：hold sb. in one's arms「把某人抱在懷中」，溫柔擁抱的意象。pick up 可為「撿起」或「抱起（小孩）」，依上下文判斷。
Para 6 ── Billy 的心聲與結局
▶He confided his worries to me. He was afraid that his little brother would have no one to play with. Also, he was scared that his dog would go hungry because he had hidden all its bones.
他把他的煩惱告訴了我。他擔心他的小弟弟會沒有人陪著玩。他也很害怕他的狗會挨餓，因為他把狗所有的骨頭都藏起來了。
句型分析 HeS confidedV his worriesO to meM。
重點：confide（v.）吐露；confide sth. to sb.「把某事告訴某人（作為秘密）」；confide in sb.「信任某人並吐露心事」。go hungry「挨餓」，go + adj. 表持續狀態。
▶It seemed that Billy knew he would never be going home. And yet, even then, he was only thinking of others.
比利似乎知道他再也不能回家了。然而，即使在那時，他也只想著別人。
句型分析 ItS（虛主詞） seemedV that Billy knew he would never be going home（真正主詞）。
重點：and yet「然而；盡管如此」，表對比轉折。even then「即使在那時」強調時間。think of others「想到他人」，展現 Billy 無私的性格。
▶When I finally stood up to leave, I gave Billy's parents my number and told them not to hesitate to call me if I could be of any assistance.
當我終於起身準備離開時，我給了比利父母我的電話號碼，並告訴他們如果我能提供任何協助，不要猶豫打給我。
句型分析 When I finally stood up to leave， IS gaveV Billy's parents my numberO（雙受詞） and toldV them not to hesitate to call me。
重點：hesitate（v.）猶豫，不要猶豫做某事 = not hesitate to V。be of assistance = be helpful（正式用法）。tell sb. not to V「告訴某人不要做某事」。
▶Two days later, I received a call from Billy's mom, who told me her son had passed away. She wanted to thank me for making a difference in his life, for he'd said to her, "Mommy, I don't mind if I can't see Santa this year because I've hugged Ronald."
兩天後，我接到比利媽媽的電話，她告訴我她的兒子已經去世了。她想感謝我讓他的生命有了意義，因為他曾對她說：「媽媽，我不介意今年見不到聖誕老人，因為我已經抱過羅納德了。」
句型分析 SheS wanted to thankV meO for making a difference in his lifeM（介系詞片語）。
重點：make a difference（to/in sb./sth.）「對某人產生影響；有意義」，為本課最重要的片語。thank sb. for + V-ing「感謝某人做了某事」。pass away = die（委婉說法）。

片語講解 × 7
片語 中文意思 例句與說明
pass on...to... 傳遞…給… Washing your hands prevents you from passing on germs to others. 洗手可防止你將細菌傳給他人。
💡 pass on 可接「訊息、細菌、知識、傳統」等，介系詞用 to。
cheer...up 使…振作；讓…高興起來 I tried to cheer her up with a funny story. 我試著用一個有趣的故事讓她振作起來。
💡 代名詞必須置中：cheer him up（✓）；cheer up him（✗）
turn...down 拒絕（請求、邀請） He turned down the job offer because the salary was too low. 他拒絕了那份工作邀約，因為薪資太低。
💡 代名詞置中：turn it down；名詞可前可後：turn down the offer / turn the offer down
be about to 即將要（做某事） I was about to leave when the phone rang. 我正要離開時，電話響了。
💡 常與 when 搭配，表「正要…，就在此時…」的轉折時間
after all 畢竟；終究 Don't give up — you've worked hard for this, after all. 別放棄——畢竟，你為此努力了很久。
💡 用作插入語時置句尾或句中（逗號隔開），表提醒某個理由；與 in the end（最終結果）意思不同
pass away 去世（die 的委婉說法） My grandfather passed away peacefully in his sleep. 我的祖父在睡眠中安詳地去世了。
💡 pass away 比 die 更溫和正式，用於表示尊重。pass on 也可表去世（口語）
make a difference 有影響；有意義；產生改變 A simple act of kindness can make a difference in someone's life. 一個簡單的善舉就能對某人的生命產生影響。
💡 make a difference (to/in sb./sth.)；make no difference（沒有差別）

文法句型 × 3
1. find + O + adj. / V-ing（感官動詞受詞補語） ▼
find + 受詞(O) + 形容詞(adj.) → 發現受詞是某種狀態 find + 受詞(O) + V-ing → 發現受詞正在做某事 find it + adj. + to V → 虛受詞結構（it 代替不定詞）
I found it tremendously rewarding to bring laughter to the kids.
我發現為孩子們帶來歡笑是極大的回報。（it = to bring...，虛受詞）
I found a small boy lying in bed.
我發現一個小男孩躺在床上。（V-ing 補語，強調進行中）
She found the movie boring.
她覺得那部電影很無聊。（adj. 補語，描述狀態）
結構重點例句
find + O + adj.O 的靜態狀態I found the door open.
find + O + V-ingO 正在進行（動態）I found him sleeping.
find it + adj. + to Vit 為虛受詞I find it hard to say no.
誤用：✗ I found that it is rewarding...（語法雖通，但此為不同句型，find + O + C 需直接接受詞補語）
同樣結構：think / consider / make + O + adj./V-ing，均可考。學測常以虛受詞 it 考句型辨識。
2. not...until...（直到…才…）與否定倒裝 ▼
S + did not + V + until + 時間/事件 → 直到…才…（直述） Not until + 時間/事件 + did + S + V → 否定倒裝（強調，正式） It was not until + 時間/事件 + that + S + V → 強調句（it...that 句型）
He did not realize the importance of rules until he broke one.
直到他違反了一條規定，他才意識到規則的重要性。（直述句）
Not until he broke a rule did he realize its importance.
直到他違反了規定，他才意識到其重要性。（否定倒裝，正式強調）
It was not until Billy stretched out his arms that the narrator decided to hug him.
直到比利伸出雙臂，敘述者才決定擁抱他。（it...that 強調句）
否定詞移至句首時，主句需助動詞倒裝（not until... did S V），勿誤寫為 not until... S did V。
學測常考：(1) not...until 直敘句填空；(2) Not until 倒裝句首選項；(3) 改寫題三種句型互換。
3. despite / in spite of（儘管…）vs. although / though ▼
despite / in spite of + N / V-ing → 介系詞，後接名詞或動名詞 although / though / even though + S + V → 連接詞，後接完整子句
Although having to turn him down pained me deeply, I had no choice.
雖然不得不拒絕他讓我深感痛苦，但我別無選擇。（although 後接子句）
Despite the strict rules, he decided to hug the boy.
儘管有嚴格的規定，他決定擁抱那個男孩。（despite + 名詞）
Despite knowing the risks, she helped the child.
儘管知道風險，她還是幫助了那個孩子。（despite + V-ing）
用詞後接例
despite / in spite ofN / V-ingdespite the rain / despite raining
although / thoughS + V（子句）although it rained
✗ despite + that 子句 → 須改為 despite the fact that + 子句（台灣學生最常犯的錯誤）
學測常考改寫：Though it was cold, they went out. → Despite the cold, they went out.（儘管寒冷，他們還是出去了。）
