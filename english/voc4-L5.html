✈️ Flight Attendant 空服員
▶Good evening, ma'am. Would you like chicken with noodles or beef with broccoli?
晚安，女士。您想要雞肉麵食，還是花椰菜牛肉呢？
✦ 句型分析 Would you like禮貌詢問 chicken with noodles or beef with broccoli選擇受詞
【禮貌問句】Would you like A or B? 是機上或餐廳點餐最常見的句型，注意用 Would you like（比 Do you want 更正式有禮）。
broccoli /ˈbrɑːkəli/ 花椰菜（綠色，也叫 green broccoli）
✈️ Sophie 蘇菲
▶I'll have the chicken with noodles, please.
我要雞肉麵食，麻煩了。
✦ 句型分析 I 'll havewill + V，表示當場決定 the chicken with noodles
【點餐說法】I'll have... 是點餐時的口語慣用語，等同 I'd like...。加上 please 更禮貌自然。
▶Ugh. This looks... awful. Why do in-flight meals always taste so bad?
噁。這看起來……糟透了。為什麼飛機餐總是這麼難吃？
✦ 句型分析 This looks awful感官連綴動詞 + 形容詞
in-flight meals taste so bad
【感官動詞】look / taste / smell / sound / feel + 形容詞（不接副詞）。
awful = very bad 極糟；in-flight meal = 飛機餐（機上膳食）
Ugh /ʌɡ/ 表示厭惡感的感嘆詞
▶No way! Are you serious? You're the first person I've ever met that likes in-flight meals!
不可能吧！你是認真的嗎？你是我遇過第一個喜歡飛機餐的人！
✦ 句型分析 You 're the first person [that likes in-flight meals]限定關係子句
【限定關係子句】the first/only/last + N + that/who + V：強調「在所有人中唯一一個…的人」。
I've ever met = 現在完成式 + ever，加強語氣（我這輩子遇到的）。
No way! 口語：不可能！/ 才不要！
▶What's so good about it?
它有什麼好的？
✦ 句型分析 What 's so good about it
What's so good about...? 質問某事有什麼值得稱讚之處，語氣帶有懷疑或不解。類似用法：What's so special about it?
▶I think the food is always overcooked and dry. Plus, the meal choices are always the same. There's nothing special about it at all!
我認為食物總是過熟且乾燥。再說，餐點選擇永遠都一樣。它根本沒有什麼特別的！
✦ 句型分析 the food is overcooked and dry並列形容詞
There 's nothing special about it at all
overcooked = 過熟的；over- 前綴表「過度」
Plus = Furthermore / In addition，連接補充說明
at all 用於否定句加強語氣 = 根本、完全
▶Alright, I guess I can do that next time. Thanks!
好吧，我想下次我可以那樣做。謝謝！
✦ 句型分析 Alright表示勉強接受 I guess [I can do that next time]受詞子句
I guess = I suppose，口語中表示「我想/我猜」，通常語氣不是很確定。
Alright 表示半推半就的接受，比 Sure! 語氣更保留。
✈️ Doris 朵麗絲
▶Really? I don't think it's all that bad. In fact, I kind of like it!
真的嗎？我不覺得有那麼差。事實上，我還蠻喜歡的！
✦ 句型分析 I don't think [it's all that bad]否定轉移
all that bad = 那麼糟（口語中 all that = so）；I don't think it's all that bad 比 I think it's not bad 更自然。
kind of = sort of，口語「有點；蠻…的」，語氣較保留
▶Ha-ha! That's funny. But I'm being perfectly honest.
哈哈！這真好笑。但我說的完全是實話。
✦ 句型分析 I 'm being perfectly honest
I'm being + adj. 用現在進行式表示「此刻正在如此表現」，與 I am honest（本性誠實）有別。
perfectly = completely / absolutely，加強語氣副詞
▶Well, it always comes out hot and fresh. And it usually tastes like home-cooked meals my mom used to make for me when I was little. How about you?
嗯，它總是熱騰騰、新鮮地端上來。而且通常嚐起來像是我媽媽以前在我小時候為我做的家常菜。你呢？
✦ 句型分析 it tastes like home-cooked meals [my mom used to make for me when I was little]關係子句
used to + V：表示過去的習慣或狀態（現在已不再）。my mom used to make meals = 我媽媽以前常常做（暗示現在不做了）。
home-cooked 家常的；come out 端上來
▶Hmm... I see. Well, maybe you can try ordering the vegetarian meal next time. I heard it's usually fresher.
嗯……我了解了。那麼，也許下次你可以試著點素食餐。我聽說通常比較新鮮。
✦ 句型分析 you can try ordering the vegetarian meal next time
try + V-ing（嘗試做某事，含實際行動）vs. try + to V（嘗試去達成，可能失敗）
vegetarian /ˌvedʒəˈteriən/ 素食的（n. 素食者）
I heard = I've heard，轉述聽來的資訊
📖 閱讀課文：Why Does Airplane Food Taste So Bad?
📖 Reading Passage 閱讀課文
▶Have you ever wondered why in-flight meals always seem to taste bland?
你是否曾好奇，為什麼飛機上的餐點嚐起來總是這麼無味？
✦ 句型分析 Have you ever wondered現在完成式疑問句 [why in-flight meals always seem to taste bland]間接問句（受詞子句）
Have you ever + p.p.? 現在完成式，詢問「有沒有曾經…的經驗」。
bland /blænd/ 無味的、清淡乏味的；wonder 好奇；思考
▶Needless to say, it's not entirely the chef's fault.
不用說，這不完全是廚師的錯。
✦ 句型分析 Needless to say副詞片語，修飾全句 it 's not entirely the chef's fault
Needless to say 不用說；當然（片語，放句首，用逗號隔開）
entirely 完全地；fault 錯誤；過失（sb.'s fault = 某人的錯）
▶For a start, the low humidity in the cabin decreases moisture in the air, which affects our taste buds and makes them less sensitive.
首先，機艙內的低濕度會減少空氣中的水分，這影響了我們的味蕾，使它們變得較不靈敏。
✦ 句型分析 For a start副詞片語 the low humidity decreases moisture
which關係代名詞，代替前句 affects our taste buds and makes them less sensitive
humidity /hjuːˈmɪdəti/ 濕度（核心字彙）；cabin 機艙
taste buds 味蕾；sensitive 靈敏的（核心字彙）
make + O + adj.：使…變得…（使役動詞句型）
▶As a result, we cannot accurately distinguish between flavors the way we usually do on the ground.
因此，我們無法像在地面上一樣準確地分辨各種味道。
✦ 句型分析 As a result結果副詞片語 we cannot accurately distinguish between flavors [the way we usually do on the ground]方式子句
accurately /ˈækjərətli/ 準確地（核心字彙）
distinguish between A and B = 區分 A 與 B（核心字彙 distinguish）
the way + S + V = 以…的方式（方式子句，等同 as）
▶In addition, the decrease in cabin pressure at high altitudes further weakens our sense of taste and smell, enabling the food to taste up to 30% less flavorful than it would on the ground.
此外，高空中機艙氣壓的降低進一步削弱了我們的味覺和嗅覺，使食物的風味比在地面上少達 30%。
✦ 句型分析 the decrease in cabin pressure weakens our sense of taste and smell
enabling分詞構句（結果），表示「導致…」 the food to taste up to 30% less flavorful
pressure /ˈpreʃər/ 壓力；氣壓（核心字彙）
enable + O + to V = 使某人/事物能夠…（核心句型）
up to 30% = 多達 30%；altitude 高度；海拔
▶Believe it or not, the loud engine noise can also distract us and change the way we perceive flavors.
信不信由你，巨大的引擎噪音也會分散我們的注意力，並改變我們感知味道的方式。
✦ 句型分析 Believe it or not插入語 the loud engine noise can distract us and change the way [we perceive flavors]
Believe it or not 信不信由你（片語，放句首）
distract /dɪˈstrækt/ 分心；使分神（核心字彙）
perceive /pərˈsiːv/ 感知；察覺
▶The condition of airplane cabins makes salty and sweet flavors harder to detect.
飛機機艙的狀況使鹹味和甜味更難被偵測到。
✦ 句型分析 The condition of airplane cabins makes salty and sweet flavors harder to detect
condition /kənˈdɪʃən/ 狀況；環境（核心字彙）
make + O + adj. 使役句型；harder to detect = 更難偵測
detect /dɪˈtekt/ 偵測；察覺
▶Some airlines have proposed solutions such as adding more spices to meals or spraying water inside the cabin to maintain moisture levels.
一些航空公司已提出解決方案，例如在餐點中加入更多香料，或在機艙內噴水以維持濕度水平。
✦ 句型分析 Some airlines have proposed solutions such as adding more spices / spraying water動名詞片語作舉例
propose /prəˈpoʊz/ 提出；建議（核心字彙）
spray /spreɪ/ 噴灑（核心字彙）；spray water 噴水
such as 例如（後面直接跟名詞或動名詞）
▶So, regardless of how well the food is prepared, these factors will always affect how it tastes.
所以，不管食物準備得多好，這些因素永遠都會影響它的味道。
✦ 句型分析 regardless of how well the food is prepared讓步副詞片語 these factors will always affect how it tastes
regardless of + N / V-ing / 疑問詞子句：不管…；無論…（核心片語）
how it tastes 間接問句作受詞；factor 因素
▶Keep this in mind the next time you complain about airline food, and look forward to a great meal when you land!
下次你抱怨飛機餐時，把這件事記在心裡，並期待降落後的美食吧！
✦ 句型分析 Keep this in mind祈使句 and look forward to a great meal when you land
keep in mind：牢記；放在心上（核心片語）
look forward to + N/V-ing：期待（核心片語，to 為介系詞後接名詞或動名詞）
land 降落；著陸

文法句型 × 3
① used to + V  ｜  過去常常（現在已不再） ▼
S + used to + V原形 例：meals my mom used to make for me when I was little
結構說明例句
used to + V過去的習慣/狀態（現在已改變）I used to hate vegetables.
be used to + V-ing/N習慣於…（現在狀態）I am used to eating alone.
be used to + V被用來做某事（被動語態）This knife is used to cut bread.
My mom used to make home-cooked meals for me every day.
我媽媽以前每天都為我做家常菜。（現在不做了）
Sophie used to enjoy eating out, but now she prefers cooking at home.
蘇菲以前喜歡外食，但現在她比較喜歡在家做菜。
✗ I am used to eat late at night.
✓ I am used to eating late at night.（be used to 後接 V-ing）
used to + V 強調「過去習慣，現在已不再」；be used to + V-ing 強調「對…習以為常」。兩者是高頻考點，務必區分！
② enable sb. to V  ｜  使某人能夠做某事 ▼
enable + 人/事物 + to + V原形 例：enabling the food to taste up to 30% less flavorful
動詞結構說明
enableenable + O + to V使某人/物能夠做某事（正面）
allow / permitallow + O + to V允許某人做某事
prevent / stopprevent + O + from V-ing阻止某人做某事
helphelp + O + (to) V幫助某人做某事
Better cabin design could enable passengers to enjoy their meals more.
更好的機艙設計能使乘客更享受他們的餐點。
Technology enables us to distinguish flavors we never noticed before.
技術使我們能夠分辨以前從未注意到的味道。
✗ enable sb. V (忘記 to)
✓ enable sb. to V
enable / allow / permit 後接「O + to V」；prevent / stop / keep 後接「O + from V-ing」，這組對比是常考句型！
③ The first/only/last + N + that/who  ｜  限定關係子句 ▼
主詞 + be + the first/only/last + N + that/who + V 例：You're the first person I've ever met that likes in-flight meals!
先行詞前的限定詞關係代名詞用法說明
the first, the only, the last只能用 that（不用 which）先行詞有 first/only/last 等最高級或序數時，慣用 that
先行詞是「人」that 或 who口語中兩者皆可
可省略受格時可省the first person (that) I've ever met
You're the first person I've ever met that likes airplane food!
你是我遇過第一個喜歡飛機餐的人！
This is the only airline that uses local ingredients in its meals.
這是唯一一家在餐點中使用當地食材的航空公司。
✗ the first person which likes... (which 不可指人)
✓ the first person who/that likes...
先行詞前有 the first / the only / the last / 序數 / 最高級時，關係代名詞慣用 that，這是高頻文法考點！
