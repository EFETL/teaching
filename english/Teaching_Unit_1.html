課文逐句精講（Reading + 翻譯）
👆 點擊任何一句英文，即可展開「中文翻譯 + 句構分析（主詞／動詞／受詞／補語）+ 重點解析」。
🔍 句構顏色對照： 主詞 S　動詞 V　受詞 O　補語 C　修飾語

本課以校刊《Longteng Senior High School News》的採訪形式呈現。記者 Jeremy 訪問三位同學，分享各自難忘的「人生第一次」經驗。

🟦 Robbie｜A First Online Class（第一次線上課）
▶A few years ago, I took an online class for the first time. I never thought that I could take classes in my pajamas!
幾年前，我第一次上了網路課。我從沒想過自己竟然可以穿著睡衣上課！
🔍 句構分析 ①A few years ago時間 IS tookV an online classO for the first time副詞
②IS never thoughtV that I could take classes in my pajamasO（名詞子句）
for the first time = 第一次（副詞片語，置於句尾）。pajamas（睡衣）恆用複數，與 jeans、scissors 同類。
▶I also enjoyed using online tools such as Kahoot! and Quizlet. There were some memorable moments, too.
我也很享受使用像 Kahoot! 和 Quizlet 這類的線上工具。也有一些令人難忘的時刻。
🔍 句構分析 ①IS enjoyedV using online tools such as Kahoot! and QuizletO（動名詞片語）
②There引導詞 wereV some memorable momentsS（真主詞）— There be 句型：動詞與真主詞 moments 一致用複數 were
enjoy + V-ing（享受做某事，後接動名詞）；such as = 例如（舉例）；memorable 為本課核心字。
▶Once, my grandmother suddenly came into the room. She yelled at me to try her perfect beef noodles.
有一次，我奶奶突然走進房間。她大聲叫我去嚐嚐她拿手的牛肉麵。
🔍 句構分析 ①Once時間 my grandmotherS cameV into the room地方副詞（不及物動詞 come，無受詞）
②SheS yelledV at me介副 to try her perfect beef noodles不定詞（表目的）
yell at sb. to V = 對某人大叫要他做某事；perfect 此處意為「完美拿手的」。
▶My microphone was on, so the rest of the students heard it and started laughing.
我的麥克風是開著的，所以其他同學都聽到了，然後開始大笑。
🔍 句構分析 ①My microphoneS wasV onC（主詞補語）— be 動詞為連綴動詞，補語說明主詞狀態
②so連接詞 the rest of the studentsS heardV itO startedV laughingO（動名詞）
the rest of = 其餘的（接複數名詞時動詞用複數）；start + V-ing / to V 皆可。
▶Another time, our teacher accidentally turned on a funny filter on her camera. She looked like an alien on the screen, so my classmates and I had another good laugh.
還有一次，我們老師不小心開了一個有趣的濾鏡。她在螢幕上看起來像個外星人，所以我和同學們又笑開了。
🔍 句構分析 ①our teacherS turned onV（片語動詞） a funny filterO on her camera副詞
②SheS lookedV like an alienC（主詞補語）— look 為連綴動詞，後接補語
③so連接詞 my classmates and IS hadV another good laughO
accidentally（不小心地）為核心字；turn on（打開電源）↔ turn off；look like + N = 看起來像。
▶Online lessons were fun sometimes, but I still found that I enjoyed seeing others in person more.
線上課程有時很有趣，但我還是發現我更喜歡親自和別人見面。
🔍 句構分析 ①Online lessonsS wereV funC（主詞補語） sometimes副詞
②but連接詞 IS foundV that I enjoyed seeing others in person moreO（名詞子句）
in person = 親自、面對面（重要片語，與 online 對比）；find that + 子句 = 發現……。
🟩 Claire｜A First Failure（第一次失敗）
▶I can still recall my first crash-and-burn moment in school. For a group presentation last year, I was the speaker for my team.
我仍然記得在學校第一次徹底慘敗的時刻。去年的一次小組報告，我是我們這組上台的人。
🔍 句構分析 ①IS can still recallV my first crash-and-burn momentO in school副詞
②For a group presentation last year副詞 IS wasV the speaker for my teamC（主詞補語）
recall（回想，直接接受詞）為核心字；crash-and-burn = 慘敗（生動口語）；speaker = 發言者、報告者。
▶However, I didn't take the task seriously and skipped several group meetings.
然而，我沒有認真看待這項任務，還翹掉了好幾次小組會議。
🔍 句構分析 ①However轉折副詞 IS didn't takeV the taskO seriously副詞
②and連接詞 skippedV several group meetingsO（與①共用主詞 I）
take...seriously = 認真對待（片語）；skip（翹掉、略過）為核心字。
▶When the time came for me to step onto the stage, I was nervous and unprepared.
輪到我上台的時候，我既緊張又毫無準備。
🔍 句構分析 ①When the time came for me to step onto the stage時間副詞子句
②IS wasV nervous and unpreparedC（主詞補語，兩個形容詞並列）— 主句
When the time came for sb. to V = 當輪到某人做……時；step onto the stage = 走上舞台；unprepared = 沒準備的（un- 否定字首）。
▶Of course, I ruined the presentation and felt embarrassed in front of my classmates and teacher.
當然，我把報告搞砸了，在同學和老師面前覺得很尷尬。
🔍 句構分析 ①IS ruinedV the presentationO
②and連接詞 feltV embarrassedC（主詞補語） in front of my classmates and teacher副詞
ruin（毀掉、搞砸）為核心字；embarrassed（人感到尷尬，-ed 結尾）為文法重點，見句型四。
▶We got a very low grade, and I felt depressed about letting the team down.
我們拿到很低的分數，我因為讓團隊失望而感到沮喪。
🔍 句構分析 ①WeS gotV a very low gradeO
②and連接詞 IS feltV depressedC（主詞補語） about letting the team down副詞（原因）
depressed（人感到沮喪，-ed）為核心字；let...down = 使……失望（片語）。
▶The failure really upset me, but I reflected on it and learned from my mistake.
這次失敗真的讓我很難過，但我反省了這件事，並從錯誤中學到教訓。
🔍 句構分析 ①The failureS really upsetV meO
②but連接詞 IS reflected onV（片語動詞） itO and連接詞 learned fromV my mistakeO
upset（使心煩，三態同形 upset-upset-upset）；reflect on = 反省（後接 on）；learn from = 從……學習。
▶These days, this memory reminds me to prepare well and make sure that I always put my team first!
這些日子以來，這段回憶提醒我要好好準備，並確保我永遠把團隊放在第一位！
🔍 句構分析 ①These days時間 this memoryS remindsV meO to prepare well and make sure that...OC（受詞補語，不定詞）— remind sb. to V：不定詞作受詞補語
remind sb. to V = 提醒某人做某事；make sure (that) = 確保；put...first = 把……放第一位。
🟧 Nancy｜A First Foreign Visit（第一次出國）
▶My first trip abroad—to Seoul, South Korea—was unforgettable.
我第一次出國旅行——去南韓首爾——令人難忘。
🔍 句構分析 ①My first trip abroadS —to Seoul, South Korea—同位補述 wasV unforgettableC（主詞補語）— 主詞核心是 trip，破折號內為插入補充
trip abroad = 出國旅行（abroad 為副詞，前不加 to）；破折號補充說明目的地；unforgettable = 難忘的。
▶The city's streets were busy, and delicious foods like Korean barbecue and kimchi were all around me.
城市的街道很熱鬧，韓式烤肉、泡菜等美食在我周圍隨處可見。
🔍 句構分析 ①The city's streetsS wereV busyC（主詞補語）
②and連接詞 delicious foods like Korean barbecue and kimchiS wereV all around meC（地方補語）
all around me = 在我周圍到處都是；like 此處 = such as（例如）。
▶I soon realized that the markets were a treat for my other senses since cheerful K-pop and colorful clothes were everywhere.
我很快就意識到，這些市場對我的其他感官來說是種享受，因為到處都是歡快的 K-pop 和繽紛的衣服。
🔍 句構分析 ①IS soon realizedV that the markets were a treat for my other sensesO（名詞子句）
②since cheerful K-pop and colorful clothes were everywhere原因副詞子句
realize that = 意識到（核心字）；a treat for the senses = 感官的享受；since = 因為（表原因）。
▶I visited several old buildings and dressed up in traditional Korean clothes.
我參觀了幾棟古老的建築，還打扮穿上了傳統的韓服。
🔍 句構分析 ①IS visitedV several old buildingsO
②and連接詞 dressed upV（片語動詞） in traditional Korean clothes副詞（與①共用主詞 I）
dress up in = 打扮穿上（某種服裝）；traditional（傳統的）為核心字。
▶While I was there, I tried to talk to locals. My Korean was poor, but fortunately, everyone was very friendly.
在那裡的時候，我試著和當地人交談。我的韓語很爛，但幸運的是，每個人都很友善。
🔍 句構分析 ①While I was there時間副詞子句 IS triedV to talk to localsO（不定詞）
②My KoreanS wasV poorC but fortunately轉折 everyoneS wasV very friendlyC
While S + was V-ing = 當……的時候（句型五）；locals = 當地人（名詞）；fortunately（幸運地）為核心字。
▶At the end of the trip, I left with a lot of great memories, and I can't wait to go back someday.
旅程結束時，我帶著許多美好的回憶離開，我等不及哪天要再回去。
🔍 句構分析 ①At the end of the trip時間 IS leftV with a lot of great memories副詞
②and連接詞 IS can't waitV to go back somedayO（不定詞）
at the end of = 在……的尾聲；can't wait to V = 等不及要做……（迫不及待）；someday = 將來某天。
📌 Jeremy 的結語
▶High school is full of firsts, and you may all have unforgettable ones like Robbie, Claire, and Nancy. Share yours with us if you have any funny, embarrassing, or wonderful experiences!
高中充滿了各種第一次，你們可能都會像 Robbie、Claire 和 Nancy 一樣，擁有難忘的經驗。如果你有任何有趣、尷尬或美好的經歷，跟我們分享吧！
🔍 句構分析 ①High schoolS isV full of firstsC（主詞補語）
②and連接詞 youS may all haveV unforgettable onesO
③ShareV（祈使句，主詞 you 省略） yoursO with us if you have any...experiences副詞 + 條件子句
be full of = 充滿；firsts = 第一次的經驗（first 當名詞可數）；ones 代替前面的 firsts。

常用俚語補充（提升口語語感）
👆 點擊每個俚語展開「意思 + 生活例句」。這些俚語與課文主題（第一次、犯錯、緊張）高度相關，能讓同學口語更道地。
crash and burn
徹底失敗、慘敗（課文出現）
▶
原指飛機墜毀起火，引申為「徹底搞砸、慘敗」。
I didn't study at all, so I totally crashed and burned on the test.我完全沒讀書，所以考試徹底考砸了。
break a leg
祝好運（尤指上台前）
▶
表面是「跌斷腿」，其實是表演、上台前的「祝你好運！」反話祝福。
Before Claire's presentation, her friend said, "Break a leg!"克萊兒上台報告前，她朋友說：「祝你好運！」

▶

a piece of cake
小事一樁；非常簡單
▶
字面「一塊蛋糕」，形容事情輕而易舉、易如反掌。
After lots of practice, speaking Korean became a piece of cake.經過大量練習後，說韓語變得輕而易舉。
spill the beans
不小心說溜嘴、洩密
▶
字面「打翻豆子」，意指不小心洩漏秘密、把事情說出去。
Robbie's open mic spilled the beans about Grandma's beef noodles.羅比沒關的麥克風把奶奶牛肉麵的事都抖了出來。
learn the ropes
熟悉門路、上手
▶
源自水手學習操控船帆繩索，引申為「學會做某事的方法、上手」。
It took me a week to learn the ropes of online classes.我花了一週才摸熟線上課程的門路。

核心句型文法解析
👆 點擊每個句型標題，展開「公式 + 例句 + 常見錯誤 + 學測重點」。
句型一｜動名詞（V-ing）當主詞▼
[ V-ing + 受詞/修飾語 ] + 單數動詞 ...

動名詞片語當主詞時，視為「一件事」，動詞用單數。

Taking online classes at home was a new experience for Robbie.
在家上網路課對羅比而言是個新體驗。
Reflecting on your mistakes is the best way to grow.
反省你的錯誤是成長最好的方式。
不可用動詞原形（Learn）或 To learning（不定詞不加 -ing）當主詞。
學測常考 V-ing 主詞 vs. to V 主詞；enjoy/avoid/finish + V-ing 也是必考。
句型二｜so + adj./adv. + that（如此…以至於）▼
S + V + so + adj./adv. + that + S + V（結果） S + V + such + a/an + adj. + N + that + S + V

表「程度高，導致某結果」。so 接形容詞/副詞，such 接名詞片語。

Nancy was so excited that she could barely sleep.
南希如此興奮，以至於幾乎睡不著。
It was such a memorable trip that she couldn't stop talking about it.
那是一趟如此難忘的旅行，以至於她講個不停。
✗ so an excited girl（so 後不接冠詞+名詞）；✓ such an excited girl。
學測常考 so / such / too / enough 辨析。
句型三｜used to vs. be used to▼
used to + V原形（過去習慣，現已不再） be / get used to + V-ing / N（習慣於）

最常被混淆的句型！差一個 be 動詞意思完全不同。

Robbie used to go to school for every class.
羅比以前每堂課都去學校上。（過去習慣）
Now he is used to studying in his pajamas.
現在他習慣穿睡衣讀書了。（已習慣）
✗ is used to go（to 後要接 V-ing 或名詞，不是原形）。
113學測綜合測驗考過 used to be 相關用法。
句型四｜情感形容詞：-ed（人）vs. -ing（事）▼
人 + feel/be + -ed 形容詞（人的感受） 事物 + be + -ing 形容詞（事物的性質）

口訣：人 + ed，事 + ing。學測最高頻文法考點之一！

人的感受（-ed）事物的性質（-ing）
embarrassed 感到尷尬embarrassing 令人尷尬的
depressed 感到沮喪depressing 令人沮喪的
excited 感到興奮exciting 令人興奮的
interested 感到有興趣interesting 有趣的
Claire felt embarrassed. / The moment was embarrassing.
克萊兒感到尷尬。／那一刻令人尷尬。
✗ I feel embarrassing. ✗ The situation was embarrassed.（兩者皆錯）
句型五｜時間副詞子句：when / while / after▼
When S + V-ed, S + V-ed.（當…時） While S + was V-ing, S + V-ed.（在…過程中） After S + V-ed, S + V-ed.（在…之後）
When the time came for me to step onto the stage, I was nervous.
當輪到我上台時，我很緊張。（課文）
While she was in Seoul, Nancy visited several old buildings.
南希在首爾期間，參觀了幾棟古老建築。
連接詞重點用法
when時間點、突發事件
while同時進行，常用進行式
after強調先後順序
113學測考 while/when/after 辨析；112學測考 despite（儘管）對比詞。
