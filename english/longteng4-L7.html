第一段｜Iceland's Global Standing
▶Iceland is a small island nation known for its breathtaking landscapes, boiling mud pools, and stunning views of the northern lights.
冰島是一個小型島國，以其令人嘆為觀止的地景、沸騰的泥漿池，以及令人驚嘆的極光景色而聞名。
句型分析 IcelandS isV a small island nationC known for its breathtaking landscapes...M（過去分詞片語，修飾 nation）
known for = 以…聞名（被動分詞片語當形容詞）。breathtaking = 令人窒息的、壯觀的。northern lights = 北極光。
▶But how many people know that this country, with fewer than 400,000 people, is also home to the world's most gender-balanced society?
但有多少人知道，這個人口不足四十萬的國家，同時也是全球性別最平衡社會的所在地？
句型分析 how many peopleS（間接疑問） knowV that this country...is also home to...O（that 名詞子句）
be home to = 是…的所在地。gender-balanced = 性別平衡的（複合形容詞，注意連字號）。with fewer than 400,000 people = 介系詞片語作插入說明。
▶For the last decade, Iceland has ranked number one on the World Economic Forum's Global Gender Gap Index.
在過去十年間，冰島一直在世界經濟論壇的全球性別差距指數中排名第一。
句型分析 For the last decadeM（時間副詞片語） IcelandS has rankedV（現在完成式：表示從過去持續到現在） number oneC
for the last decade 搭配現在完成式，強調「至今已持續十年」。World Economic Forum = 世界經濟論壇（WEF）。Gender Gap Index = 性別差距指數。
▶The path to equality began on October 24, 1975, when 90% of the female population participated in the "Women's Day Off" to protest against wage inequality and other gender-related social injustices.
通往平等的道路始於一九七五年十月二十四日，當天有九成的女性人口參加了「婦女休假日」，以抗議薪資不平等及其他與性別相關的社會不公義。
句型分析 The path to equalityS beganV on October 24, 1975M（時間） when 90% of the female population participated...M（關係副詞 when 引導定語子句，修飾 1975）
protest against = 抗議（反對）。wage inequality = 薪資不平等。gender-related = 與性別相關的（複合形容詞）。注意 to protest 是不定詞片語表目的。
第二段｜The Strike Day
▶That day, instead of going to work, cooking meals, looking after children, or doing domestic chores, Icelandic women hoping for change took to the streets in protest.
那一天，冰島婦女不去上班、不煮飯、不照顧孩子，也不做家事，而是滿懷改變的期望走上街頭抗議。
句型分析 That dayM（時間） instead of going to work, cooking meals, looking after children, or doing domestic choresM（instead of + 動名詞並列） Icelandic women hoping for changeS（+ 分詞片語修飾） took to the streetsV（片語：走上街頭）
instead of + V-ing = 而不是…（對比）。take to the streets = 走上街頭（示威）。domestic chores = 家務雜事。
▶With the majority of women going on strike, the country fell into complete chaos.
隨著大多數女性參加罷工，整個國家陷入了完全的混亂。
句型分析 With the majority of women going on strikeM（with + N + V-ing 獨立分詞構句，表示伴隨/因果狀態） the countryS fell intoV（片語動詞） complete chaosO
with + N + V-ing = 因為…（介系詞構句，強調因果關係）。go on strike = 罷工。fall into chaos = 陷入混亂。
▶Banks and factories came to a standstill as many of their employees were women, flights were grounded because there was no one to provide service, and without actresses, theaters had to cancel performances.
銀行和工廠陷入停頓，因為大部分員工都是女性；航班停飛，因為沒有人提供服務；而少了女演員，劇院不得不取消演出。
句型分析（三個並列主句） ① Banks and factoriesScame to a standstillV（片語：陷入停頓）
② flightsSwere groundedV（被動）
③ theatersShad to cancelVperformancesO
come to a standstill = 陷入停頓、停擺。be grounded = 停飛（被動）。without + N = 少了…（介系詞片語）。
▶Additionally, mothers striking all day long simply left children in the care of their husbands.
此外，整天罷工的母親們乾脆將孩子托付給丈夫照顧。
句型分析 AdditionallyM（連接副詞） mothers striking all day longS（+ 現在分詞片語修飾） leftV childrenO in the care of their husbandsM
in the care of = 由…照料（固定片語）。striking all day long = 現在分詞片語修飾 mothers。additionally = 此外（轉承語）。
▶Since the mainly female-run schools and nurseries were closed, fathers were thus forced to take their children with them to work.
由於主要由女性管理的學校和托兒所關閉，父親們因此被迫帶著孩子一起去上班。
句型分析 Since the mainly female-run schools and nurseries were closedM（Since 原因副詞子句） fathersS were forced to takeV（被動 + 不定詞） their childrenO
female-run = 由女性經營的（複合形容詞）。nursery = 托兒所。be forced to V = 被迫去做某事。
▶Some men bought candy and colored pencils in a desperate effort to have their children entertained, while others offered rewards to their older kids to have them take care of the younger ones.
有些男性拼命買糖果和彩色鉛筆，好讓孩子有事可做；而其他人則給年長的孩子獎勵，讓他們照顧年幼的弟妹。
句型分析 Some menS boughtV in a desperate effort to have their children entertainedM（不定詞片語表目的）
while others offered rewards...（while 對比副詞子句）
have + O + p.p. = 使受詞被…（使役動詞，被動語意）。in a desperate effort to = 拼命試圖…。while 此處表對比（而另一方面）。
▶This protest paralyzed the entire nation.
這場抗議使整個國家陷入癱瘓。
句型分析 This protestS paralyzedV the entire nationO
paralyze (v.) = 使癱瘓、使麻痺。此句為段落總結，強調衝擊之大。entire = 整個的（強調語氣）。
第三段｜Results & Legislation
▶However, if it had not been for this protest, public opinion would not have changed and support for gender equality would not have grown.
然而，若不是這場抗議，民意就不會改變，對性別平等的支持也不會提升。
句型分析（與過去事實相反的假設語氣） 條件子句：if it had not been for this protest（過去完成式條件）
主要子句①：public opinionSwould not have changedV（would + have + p.p.）
主要子句②：support for gender equalitySwould not have grownV
if it had not been for = 若不是因為（與過去事實相反假設）；可倒裝為 Had it not been for...。主要子句用 would/could/might + have + p.p.。
▶One year later, Iceland established the Gender Equality Council and passed the Gender Equality Act, banning gender discrimination in the workplace and in schools.
一年後，冰島成立了性別平等委員會，並通過了《性別平等法》，禁止職場和學校中的性別歧視。
句型分析 One year laterM IcelandS established...and passedV（並列動詞） banning gender discrimination...M（現在分詞構句，說明法律內容）
establish = 成立、建立。pass (v.) = 通過（法律）。ban (v.) = 禁止。discrimination (n.) = 歧視。
▶Then in 1980, an even bigger triumph was achieved when Iceland elected the world's first female president of a democratic country.
接著在一九八〇年，冰島選出了全球民主國家中的第一位女性總統，這是一個更大的勝利。
句型分析 an even bigger triumphS was achievedV（被動） when Iceland elected the world's first female president of a democratic countryM（when 時間副詞子句）
even + 比較級 = 更加…（修飾比較級）。triumph = 勝利、成功。democratic (adj.) = 民主的；democracy (n.)。
第四段｜Continued Struggle
▶However, despite all the progress the country had made, the fight for gender equality was not yet over.
然而，儘管這個國家已取得了所有進步，爭取性別平等的鬥爭尚未結束。
句型分析 despite all the progress the country had madeM（despite + 名詞片語，表讓步） the fight for gender equalityS wasV not yet overC
despite + N/V-ing = 儘管（介系詞，後不接子句）；對比 although + 子句（連接詞）。not yet = 還未。
▶In 2005, Iceland's gap showed that women, on average, still being paid only 64.15% of what men earned.
二〇〇五年，冰島的差距顯示，女性平均薪資仍只有男性的 64.15%。
句型分析 Iceland's gapS showedV that women...still being paid only 64.15% of what men earnedO（that 名詞子句）
on average = 平均而言（插入語）。what men earned = 名詞子句作介系詞 of 的受詞（what = the amount that）。
▶So, on the thirtieth anniversary of the Women's Day Off, women organized another strike. They left work at 2:08 p.m.—the exact time at which they started working for free compared with men.
因此，在婦女休假日三十週年之際，女性們再次發起罷工。她們在下午兩點零八分離開工作崗位——這正是相較於男性，她們開始免費工作的確切時刻。
句型分析 on the thirtieth anniversary of the Women's Day OffM（時間副詞片語） TheyS leftV at 2:08 p.m.—the exact time at which they started working for free compared with menM（破折號引導同位語）
anniversary = 週年紀念日。at which = when（關係副詞子句，前有介系詞）。work for free = 免費工作。compared with = 與…相比。
▶In 2016, women in Iceland again left work early, this time at 2:38 p.m. This was to remind people that the average woman still earned just 70.42% of the salary that males usually given to men.
二〇一六年，冰島女性再度提早離開工作，這次是下午兩點三十八分。目的是提醒人們，一般女性的薪資仍只相當於男性通常所得薪資的 70.42%。
句型分析 ThisS wasV to remind people that...C（不定詞片語作主詞補語）
remind sb. that = 提醒某人（接子句）。此段強調薪酬差距改善的緩慢。每年罷工的「提早離開時間」是衡量薪資差距的直觀方式。
第五段｜Legal Reform
▶Finally, Iceland decided to get tough on inequality. In 2018, it became the first country to legally demand that men and women be paid equally.
最終，冰島決定對不平等問題採取強硬手段。二〇一八年，它成為第一個以法律規定男女同酬的國家。
句型分析 itS becameV the first country to legally demandC（不定詞片語作後置修飾） that men and women be paid equallyO（demand 後接假設語氣：should be 或原形 be）
get tough on = 對…採取嚴厲措施。demand that + S + (should) + V原形 = 要求…（假設語氣，學測常考句型）！
▶The revolutionary new law required that companies with at least twenty-five employees obtain a certificate proving that they were following national equal pay policies; otherwise, they would face heavy fines.
這項革命性的新法律規定，員工達二十五人以上的公司，必須取得證明其遵循國家同工同酬政策的證書；否則將面臨高額罰款。
句型分析 The revolutionary new lawS required that companies...obtainV（require + that 假設語氣） ; otherwise, they would face heavy fines（分號後為結果子句）
require that + S + (should) + V原形（假設語氣）。certificate = 證書。otherwise = 否則（轉承語）。fine (n.) = 罰款。
第六段｜Conclusion
▶Today, this Nordic wonderland has some of the world's strictest laws on workplace equality.
如今，這個北歐奇境擁有全球最嚴格的職場平等法律。
句型分析 this Nordic wonderlandS hasV some of the world's strictest laws on workplace equalityO
some of the world's strictest = 全球數一數二嚴格的（not 最高級，保留謙遜語氣）。Nordic = 北歐的。wonderland = 奇境、仙境。
▶Despite having achieved so much, the country continues to take bold steps to eliminate gender discrimination and become a fairer society where every person is treated equally.
儘管已取得如此多的成就，這個國家仍持續採取大膽措施，以消除性別歧視，成為一個人人平等對待的更公平社會。
句型分析 Despite having achieved so muchM（despite + 動名詞片語） the countryS continues to takeV bold steps to eliminate gender discrimination...O（不定詞片語表目的）
despite + having + p.p. = 儘管已…（despite 後不接子句）。take bold steps = 採取大膽措施。eliminate = 消除、根除。
▶But for Iceland's marvelous efforts in this regard, the rest of the world would not have such a wonderful role model to follow.
若不是冰島在這方面的了不起努力，世界上其他地方就不會有如此美好的榜樣可以效仿。
句型分析（但書式假設語氣） But for Iceland's marvelous efforts in this regardM（But for = If it were not for，與現在事實相反假設） the rest of the worldS would not haveV（would + V） such a wonderful role model to followO
But for + N = 若非有…（= If it were not for / If it had not been for，依上下文決定時態）。in this regard = 在這方面。marvelous = 了不起的。role model = 榜樣。

📐
文法句型 × 3
1. 假設語氣：If it had not been for...（與過去事實相反） ▼
條件子句：If it had not been for + N, ... 倒裝形式：Had it not been for + N, ... 主要子句：S + would / could / might + have + p.p.

此句型表示「若不是因為某事（過去已發生）」，暗示若沒有該事，結果會不同。條件子句用「過去完成式」，主要子句用「would/could/might + have + p.p.」。

If it had not been for this protest, public opinion would not have changed.
若不是這場抗議，民意就不會改變。
Had it not been for Iceland's laws, other countries might not have followed.
若非冰島的法律，其他國家可能不會跟進。（倒裝形式）
But for their courage, the movement would not have succeeded.
若非她們的勇氣，這場運動就不會成功。（But for = If it had not been for）
條件子句主要子句
If it had not been for + N（過去）would/could/might + have + p.p.
If it were not for + N（現在）would/could/might + V原形
But for + N（現在或過去均可）依語境選擇時態
常見錯誤：條件子句用 hadn't been（正式寫作應用 had not been）；主要子句誤用 would not change（應加 have）。
學測超考：此句型常出現在閱讀測驗選項，考「假設語氣時態判斷」。If...had + p.p. → 主句 would + have + p.p.（過去式假設）。
2. With + N + V-ing（分詞獨立構句，伴隨/因果狀態） ▼
With + 名詞（片語）+ 現在分詞（V-ing）, 主句... （表示「在…正在發生的情況下」或「由於…」）

「With + N + V-ing」是一種分詞構句，用介系詞 with 引導，表達「在…正在發生的狀態下」或「由於…」，常用於說明背景原因。

With the majority of women going on strike, the country fell into complete chaos.
隨著大多數女性走上罷工，全國陷入完全的混亂。
With more companies following the equal pay law, the gender gap is narrowing.
隨著更多公司遵循同工同酬法，性別差距正在縮小。
結構意思例子
With + N + V-ing正在…（主動）With women striking
With + N + p.p.被…（被動）With the law passed
With + N + adj.呈…狀態With the door open
常見錯誤：誤用 With + N + V原形（❌ With women strike）；V-ing 表主動，p.p. 表被動，需根據名詞與動作的關係判斷。
學測：此構句常出現在克漏字或句子合併題，考「分詞的主被動判斷」。
3. despite + N/V-ing vs. although + 子句（讓步關係比較） ▼
despite / in spite of + 名詞 / V-ing（介系詞） although / even though / though + 完整子句（連接詞）

「despite」和「although」都表示讓步（儘管），但文法結構不同：despite 是介系詞，後接名詞/動名詞；although 是連接詞，後接完整主謂子句。

Despite all the progress the country had made, the fight was not yet over.
儘管這個國家已取得所有進步，戰鬥還沒有結束。（despite + 名詞片語）
Despite having achieved so much, the country continues to take bold steps.
儘管已取得如此多成就，這個國家仍持續採取大膽措施。（despite + V-ing）
Although Iceland has achieved gender equality in many areas, some gaps remain.
儘管冰島在許多領域已實現性別平等，仍存在一些差距。（although + 子句）
詞類後接結構錯誤用法
despite / in spite ofN / V-ing / the fact that...❌ despite + 子句（直接）
although / though完整子句（S + V）❌ although + N（無動詞）
常見錯誤：❌ despite it was raining → ✓ despite the rain / although it was raining
學測超頻：克漏字常考 despite/although 互換，判斷關鍵：後面是名詞/V-ing？選 despite；後面是完整子句（有主詞+動詞）？選 although。
